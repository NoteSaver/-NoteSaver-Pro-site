Frequently Asked Questions

Is NoteSaver Pro really free?
Yes! The core features of NoteSaver Pro are completely free — you can create, edit, and save notes without paying anything. Premium features like PDF/DOCX export and advanced organization are available with a paid plan.
How do I download my notes as PDF?
PDF and DOCX export is a Premium feature. Upgrade your account to unlock instant downloads in professional formats. Go to Dashboard → select a note → click Export.
Is my data secure on NoteSaver Pro?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS. Your passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We never read or sell your notes to third parties.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your Settings page. Your premium access will continue until the end of the billing period. We offer a 7-day refund policy if you're not satisfied.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
